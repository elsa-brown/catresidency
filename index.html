CAT Residency

CAT Residency is a residency program open to anyone.
To participate:

  1. Identify an amenable household in need of a house,
    cat, or other pet sitter.

  2. Locate a friendly individual who will sublet your current
    home during the length of the residency (if applicable).

  3. Make arrangements.

  4. Move in to the appointed CAT Residency, and use this
    time and space as you like.

For inquiries or advice, contact CATResidency@gmail.com.
