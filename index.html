CAT Residency

CAT Residency is an open source artist residency program.

To participate:

  1. Identify an amenable household in need of a house, cat, or other pet sitter.

  2. Locate a friendly individual who will sublet your current home during the length of the residency (if applicable).

  3. Make arrangements.

  4. Move in to the appointed CAT Residency, and use this time and space as you like.

For more information contact CATResidency@gmail.com.
